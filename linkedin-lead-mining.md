# LinkedIn Lead Mining with Apify (BYOK)

Mine B2B leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator using your own Apify key.

## Why LinkedIn Data Matters

Google Maps is great for local businesses. LinkedIn is where you find:
- SaaS decision-makers
- Agency owners and executives  
- Professional services (lawyers, consultants, accountants)
- Enterprise buyers

## How It Works in Suplex

1. Get an [Apify account](https://apify.com) (free tier available)
2. Create an API key
3. Paste it into Suplex settings (BYOK = Bring Your Own Key)
4. Suplex uses Apify's LinkedIn scrapers at their published rates — no markup

## What You Get Per Lead

From LinkedIn profiles:
- Full name
- Job title and company
- Work email (when available)
- Company size and industry
- Profile URL

Suplex enriches this further:
- Company website analysis
- Tech stack detection
- Additional contact discovery

## Cost

Apify charges ~$0.01-0.05 per LinkedIn profile depending on depth.
Suplex adds zero markup — you pay Apify directly.

Compare to:
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator: $99-149/month for limited exports
- ZoomInfo: $15,000+/year
- Apollo credits: $0.30-0.50 per contact

## Lead Quality

LinkedIn leads are high-intent for B2B:
- Verified professionals (real people, current jobs)
- Company context included
- Better email match rates vs. purchased lists

## Best Practices

1. **Target narrow segments** — "Marketing Directors at 50-200 person SaaS companies in Austin" beats "all marketers"
2. **Verify before sending** — Suplex verifies emails automatically
3. **Respect rate limits** — Apify handles this, but don't run too many concurrent scrapes
4. **Combine with Google Maps** — local businesses on Maps, B2B executives on LinkedIn
