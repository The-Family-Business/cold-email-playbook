# Using Cold Email Leads for Meta Ads Custom Audiences

Your verified cold email leads are also your best Meta Ads targeting data.

## The Multi-Touch Strategy

Same audience. Multiple channels.

1. Mine leads with Suplex (Google Maps + LinkedIn)
2. Cold email them with personalized sequences  
3. Export the same list → Meta Ads Custom Audience
4. Run retargeting ads to people you're emailing
5. Create Lookalike Audiences from your best leads

Result: prospects encounter your brand across email + Facebook + Instagram before they ever reply.

## Why Lead Quality Matters for Meta Matching

Meta matches your uploaded emails to Facebook profiles. Match rate determines everything.

- Stale purchased lists: 10-20% match rate
- Fresh, verified Suplex leads: 40-60% match rate

Suplex verifies every email before it enters your database. Higher verification rate = higher Meta match rate = better audience quality.

## Exporting from Suplex

Suplex is a desktop app. Your data lives in a local SQLite database on your machine.

Export process:
1. Filter leads by campaign, quality, or any criteria
2. Export to CSV
3. Upload to Meta Ads Manager → Audiences → Customer List

30 seconds. Your data, no permission required.

## Lookalike Audiences

Upload your highest-quality leads as a Meta Lookalike seed:
- Meta analyzes their behavior patterns and interests
- Builds a Lookalike Audience of millions with the same profile
- 500 quality leads → potential audience of 500,000+

Seed quality is everything. Enriched, verified, targeted leads = strong Lookalike signal.
