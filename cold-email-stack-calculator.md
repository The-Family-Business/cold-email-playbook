## Why Is Suplex So Much Cheaper?

Three reasons:

**1. Desktop app, not cloud SaaS**  
Enrichment runs on your computer. No servers to pay for. No infrastructure costs to pass on.

**2. BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys)**  
You pay Apify for LinkedIn data, OpenAI for AI writing. Suplex doesn't mark up these costs — you pay the providers directly at their published rates.

**3. Your email accounts = free sending**  
Gmail and Outlook accounts cost nothing. Suplex routes sends through accounts you already have. No "per seat" sending fees.

## What You Give Up

Honest comparison:

- **Apollo's 275M database**: Suplex mines live data from Google Maps and LinkedIn. Smaller global coverage, but often more current for local B2B.

- **Unlimited mailboxes (Instantly/Smartlead)**: Standard Suplex volume is 40/day per account. For 2,500+/day, integrate with Smartlead.
- **Multi-channel (phone/LinkedIn automation)**: Suplex is email-focused.

For most small-mid businesses and solo operators: the trade-offs are worth it.
